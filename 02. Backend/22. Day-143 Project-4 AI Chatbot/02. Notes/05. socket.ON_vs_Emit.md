## Extra Tips:

* Both **client and server** can use `emit` and `on`.
* You can emit events to:

  * **One socket** (`socket.emit`)
  * **All sockets** (`io.emit`)
  * **Rooms or namespaces** (advanced)
* Always agree on **event names** and **data formats** between client and server.
